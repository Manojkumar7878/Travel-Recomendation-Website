Help & FAQs

You can book a ticket by entering the source, destination, and travel date in our search system on the home page. Select your preferred bus and proceed to booking.

We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI payments, and all major digital wallets including PhonePe, GPay, and Paytm.

You can cancel your ticket by logging into your account, going to "Book Ticket" and clicking Cancel next to your booking.

Refunds depend on the cancellation policy of the bus operator. Please check the refund policy before canceling your booking.
