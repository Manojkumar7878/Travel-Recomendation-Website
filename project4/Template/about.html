About Us

HumBus Travels - Your Trusted Travel Partner Since 1974

Our Journey

Our founder believed that customer satisfaction is the key to success in the travel and tourism sector. He wanted his company to earn loyal customers and invested heavily in bringing them comfort. His taxi was special in 1967 because of his hospitality to his customers. He never wanted his customers to keep a travel a distant memory. Instead, he wanted it to be a refreshing one and made it available to every passenger who seeks our services.

Our Vision

To be a trendsetter in the tourism and travel industry. To be the behemoth in service excellence. Through exceptional service, we want our customers to realize the value of their money spent and make our brand a household name among satisfied travelers.

Our Mission

The traditional motto of the company, 'Service before oneself', is followed at all levels of management, emphasizing customer comfort at every stage of business. The core of every travel and tourism service is customer satisfaction.
